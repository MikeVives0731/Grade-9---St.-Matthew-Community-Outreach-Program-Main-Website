Serving Smiles: St. Matthew's Community Outreach

Welcome to Serving Smiles, a service that
provides canteen workers with happiness as
they get to enjoy our program today. Our goal
is to provide happiness towards the canteen
workers of Marist School Marikina. We have
invited 15 canteen workers to participate
in our community outreach. So come and join us as
we help these workers become happy.

Our objectives

Showing appreciation to our canteen workers.
Giving happiness and appreciating their efforts.
And creating a stronger relationship between students and canteen workers.
